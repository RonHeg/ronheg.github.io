About Me

I’m a Senior Embedded Systems Architect with over four decades of experience delivering production-ready hardware and firmware solutions across medical, industrial, aerospace, and IoT industries.

My work includes mixed-signal and analog design, low-EMI power systems, firmware for STM32/ESP32/ATSAMD platforms, wireless integrations (LoRa, WiFi, Bluetooth), and regulatory-compliant product development (Class II medical devices).

I also partner with Five Design & Development LLC to provide multidisciplinary engineering support from concept through release.

Services

  • Low-EMI / High-Reliability Design – Power electronics, switching supplies, EMI mitigation
  • Regulatory & Compliance Consulting – Medical device documentation, testing strategy
  • Wireless & IoT Systems – LoRa, WiFi, Bluetooth, antenna tuning
  • Firmware Development – C, STM32, ESP32, ATSAMD, low-power optimization
  • Technical Mentorship & Review – Architectural reviews and mentoring for engineering teams
